The Main Thesis
The Space Between Human and Machine Emotion

We're at an inflection where AI cognition research, human biometric capture, and cultural behavioral data are mature enough to build something genuinely new.

The question isn't just "what do humans feel" or "what do AI models simulate feeling" — it's what happens in the space between them, and how do we measure, interpret, and design for that.

An HMI emotional symbiosis framework that could be both a research platform and a client-facing service offering.
